Our Vision

At About Fitness, we believe that fitness should be accessible, modern, and digitized.

We are a state-of-the-art gym management system designed to modernize gym operations. Our platform bridges the gap between gym owners and members by digitizing receipts, automating notifications, and providing a structured platform for fitness management.

With Firebase as our backbone, we ensure portability, maintainability, and extensibility for future enhancements like supplement stores and nutrition advisory modules.
